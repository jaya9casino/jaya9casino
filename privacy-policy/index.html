Information We May Collect

Jaya9 may collect information connected to registration, login, verification, payments, gameplay and support requests. This can include contact details, account identifiers, transaction records, device data and communication history when a player asks for help.

How Information Is Used

Collected information is used to operate the account, process payments, manage bonuses, maintain security, detect unusual activity and respond to support requests. Some data is also needed to keep the platform stable and improve navigation across the site and app.

Security controls are used to reduce unauthorised access, but players also need to protect their own accounts. Use strong passwords, keep OTP codes private and contact support if you notice activity you do not recognise.
